# Moon Studios Local SEO Playbook

## Positioning summary

Moon Studios should win on a mix of:
- warm, family-friendly experience
- specialist services people actively search for
- strong social proof from Google reviews
- honest local targeting around Andheri East and Marol
- nearby-area capture using "near Powai", "near Ghatkopar", and "near Kurla" phrasing

Repeated themes:
- warmth and friendliness

This is the language to repeat across:
- Google Business Profile description

## Review response angle

Keep replies short and human. Reuse these themes:
- thank them for trusting the team
- mention the exact service when possible
- reflect warmth and comfort
- invite them back for the next maintenance visit

Example:

Thank you so much for trusting us for your Nanoplastia treatment. We are really happy you felt comfortable with the team and loved the result. Looking forward to welcoming you back to Moon Studios again soon.
